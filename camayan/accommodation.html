Welcome to Camayan Beach Resort

Camayan Beach Resort is a special place. Located at the water’s edge, it provides the perfect blend of peace, tranquility and beauty. An enjoyable and stress-free vacation awaits guests who stay in one of the 44 Superior Rooms, 38 Deluxe Rooms or the 3 spacious Suites. All rooms offer a comfortable lifestyle in harmony with nature. Enjoy a spectacular view of the tropical forest and picturesque bay. Watch incredible sunsets and catch the ocean breezes all year long.

Accommodation

 a. Superior and De Luxe Room

     All our Superior and Deluxe rooms come equipped with two queen sized beds and are ideal for families, parents with up to three children or for entertaining fellow guests. Enjoy views of the forest and bay, pleasant terrace, mini-bar, satellite TV, air-conditioning, and complimentary wireless internet (wifi). With larger area and individual terrace, our Deluxe rooms offer more exclusivity for our guests.

 b. Suitebed

     The Suites with separate bedroom and living room is perfect for informal meetings or if you simply want a comfortable and lovely space to stretch out and relax. Families and couples alike have found the Suite perfect for their needs. The Captain’s Suite has a comfortable king-size bed and offers a spectacular view of the sea and garden. Room amenities include a jacuzzi, mini-bar, free wireless internet (wi-fi), coffee & tea maker, large walk-in closet, dining room, and kitchenette.
