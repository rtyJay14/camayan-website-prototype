Welcome to Camayan Beach Resort

Camayan Beach Resort is a special place. Located at the water’s edge, it provides the perfect blend of peace, tranquility and beauty. An enjoyable and stress-free vacation awaits guests who stay in one of the 44 Superior Rooms, 38 Deluxe Rooms or the 3 spacious Suites. All rooms offer a comfortable lifestyle in harmony with nature. Enjoy a spectacular view of the tropical forest and picturesque bay. Watch incredible sunsets and catch the ocean breezes all year long.

Packages

 a. Dives Plus

Dives Plus

     Build your own dive adventure by choosing up to 3 dives from the many opportunities we offer. Be creative! Start with a Dolphin Dive Encounter and then explore a shipwreck and finish with a coral reef. Plan it yourself, or we’ll design a dive package for you.

Adventure Club Dives

     For Adventure Club members only – very special rates for your dive club when you buy 40 dives at one time. Boat dives require 4 divers, shore dives available for 2 or more divers.

 b. Wreck1

     Scuba diving on an old shipwreck is an adventure and each wreck is unique. Subic Bay has 9 different ships to explore. Choose your experience based depth of dive, historical period of interest, and the marine life you want to see. We’ll be happy to make recommendations, if desired. Each dive begins with a short presentation on the wreck you’ll be exploring and what special features you should look for.

Please call our Dive Programs Coordinator at (047) 252-8000 for more information.

 c. Wreck2

     Coral reefs throughout the world are disappearing at a frightening rate. The Philippines, one of the richest coral reef islands, has only 15% of its reefs remaining. Ocean Adventure is working to protect and rebuild coral reefs in Subic Bay and we offer 4 different reef dives – Grande Island, Minangga Reef, Chiquita Island, and Nabasan Reef. Each dive begins with a brief description of the corals, plants, fishes, and invertebrates you’ll be seeing to enhance the experience.

Prices and Packages

for Shipwreck and Marinelife Dives: First dive Php1,400, additional dives Php 1,120 Includes park admission, scuba tank and weight belt, mask, snorkel, fins, booties, BCD, regulator, and wetsuit. And don’t forget your dive certification card to show at registration.

Please call our Dive Programs Coordinator at (047) 252-8000 for more information./p>

 d. Dolphin Dive

     Join our whales and dolphins in their natural, open water lagoon for a special, intimate, underwater experience you’ll never forget. Swim, explore, and pose with our cetacean friends. A unique opportunity to interact with these amazing animals in their home environment filled with fish, corals, and white sand.

Price and Package:

Php 4,760 includes park admission, scuba tank and weight belt, mask, snorkel, fins, booties, BCD, regulator, and wetsuit. And don’t forget your dive certification card to show at registration.

Please call our Dive Programs Coordinator at (047) 252-8000 for more information.
